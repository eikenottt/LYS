Basement

Basements often have no natural light, making it very important with good lighting no matter what you use it for. Because of the fact that there are no lights except what you introduce with bulbs, you will need stronger lighting here than many other places. Recommended light strength and color for basements are:
5000-1000Total lumen, depending on size of the room Bright white: 5000kelvin, to get a white light color to illuminate the room.

Suitable light

  1. LED
  2. HID (High Intensity Discharge)
